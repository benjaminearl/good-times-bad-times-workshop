Exercise 1 (homework): One Minute Recording

Bring a one minute sound recording that represents “a space” to class.


Exercise 2: Thinking through Talking

Team up with someone else. Go somewhere quiet (or not) and interview each other for 15 minutes about what you would want to “make space for” using radio. Record the conversation with your phone’s voice recorder.


Exercise 3: Create a 5 min radio show

Send your recordings of these conversations to another pair in the group. Once you receive somebody else’s recordings, listen through and try to extract a core idea from it to develop into a 5 min sketch for a show. This could be a talk or music show, some experimental recordings interviews, a play, anything.

(Also think of what could be a visual and jingle for this show!)
